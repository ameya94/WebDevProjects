Divino FoodWorks Limited (the Company) is a Divino Bhartia Group Company,The Company was incorporated in
1994 andinitiated operations in 1996,The Company got listed on the Indian bourses in February 2010,Mr Shyam
S Bhartia,Mr Hari S Bhartia and Divino Enpro Private Ltd are the Promoters of the Company.The Company & its
subsidiary operates Fresco Gustoso Pizza brand with the exclusive rights for India,Nepal,Bangladesh and

500+ Fresco Gustoso Pizza restaurants.

or else FREE to a community of loyal consumers from all our restaurants around the country.

Fresco Gustosos vision is focused on "Exceptional people on a mission to be the best pizza delivery company
